**1) Personal Experience**
-What kind of accommodation do you usually choose when you travel?
-Have you ever stayed in a guesthouse? How was it?
-Have you ever used Airbnb or a similar service?
-What’s the most expensive accommodation in your life?
-Have you ever complained to the front desk? What happened?

**2) Comfort and Essentials**
-What items do you always check for in a hotel room?
-What annoys you the most in a poorly maintained room?
-What small details make a room feel luxurious to you?
-Would you rather stay at a hotel with a great view or in a perfect location?

**3) Personal Preferences**
-Would you stay in a place without a TV or internet?
-What would your dream hotel room look like?
-If you had enough money, would you prefer staying alone in a hotel or sharing it with your friends?
-Would you try a homestay with a local family?
