1️⃣ **Travel & Adventure Goals**

**-Which country do you most want to visit before you die?**
head to → ~로 향하다
set off → 출발하다
wanderlust → 여행욕구
curiosity → 호기심
on my bucket list → 꼭 해보고 싶은 목록에 있는

_“Visiting Iceland has always been on my bucket list because of its surreal landscapes.”_

**-What’s the most adventurous thing you’ve ever done?**
take on → 도전하다
get through → 해내다
thrill → 스릴
courage → 용기
push one’s limits → 한계를 뛰어넘다

_“Skydiving helped me push my limits and realize how strong fear can make us.”_

**-Do you prefer solo travel or traveling with others?**
hang out with → 함께 어울리다
set out → 떠나다
companionship → 동행
solitude → 고독
fly solo → 혼자 행동하다

_“Traveling alone taught me to fly solo and discover who I truly am.”_

**-Would you rather explore nature or big cities?**
look around → 둘러보다
get away from → 벗어나다
skyscraper → 고층 건물
atmosphere → 분위기
off the beaten path → 잘 알려지지 않은 길

_“I prefer to travel off the beaten path and find hidden gems away from crowds.”_

2️⃣ **Career & Skill Goals**

**-What’s your ultimate career goal?**
work toward → 목표를 향해 노력하다
move up → 승진하다
promotion → 승진
dedication → 헌신
climb the corporate ladder → 회사에서 승진의 사다리를 오르다

_“I’m working toward a leadership role because I’ve always wanted to climb the corporate ladder.”_

**-What kind of skills do you want to master in your lifetime?**
work on → 향상시키다
build up → 쌓다
proficiency → 숙련도
self-improvement → 자기계발
jack of all trades→ 다방면에 능숙한 사람

_“I want to be a jack of all trades—able to adapt and learn new skills whenever needed.”_

**-Is starting your own business on your bucket list?**
start up → 창업하다
deal with → 처리하다
innovation → 혁신
strategy → 전략
take the plunge → 과감히 시작하다

_“One day, I’d love to take the plunge and start up my own company built on passion.”_

**-What kind of mentor or role model inspires you the most?**
look up to → 존경하다
count on → 의지하다
inspiration → 영감
wisdom → 지혜
a guiding light→ 인생의 길잡이

_“My mentor has always been a guiding light who helps me see my potential clearly.”_

3️⃣ **Relationship & Personal Connection Goals**

**-What kind of person do you hope to spend your life with?**
settle down with → 함께 정착하다
care for → 돌보다
companionship → 동반자 관계
affection → 애정
a match made in heaven → 천생연분

_“I hope to settle down with someone who feels like a match made in heaven.”_

**-Do you have any friendship goals on your bucket list?**
reach out to → 연락하다
open up to → 마음을 열다
connection → 유대감
sincerity → 진정성
through thick and thin → 좋을 때나 나쁠 때나 함께

_“True friends are those who stay with you through thick and thin.”_

**-Who has had the greatest emotional impact on your life?**
rely on → 의지하다
bring out → 이끌어내다
gratitude → 감사
empathy → 공감
leave a mark on someone → 누군가에게 깊은 인상을 남기다

_“My mother left a mark on me by showing what true empathy and strength look like.”_

**-What kind of person do you want to become for others?**
stand by → 지지하다
lift up → 격려하다
kindness → 친절
integrity → 진정성
be someone’s rock → 누군가의 든든한 버팀목이 되다

_“I want to be someone’s rock—steady, dependable, and full of warmth.”_

**On the process**
진행 중인, 과정에 있는
The project is still on the process, so we can’t share the results yet.

**Apply for**
~에 지원하다
She decided to apply for a scholarship to study abroad.

**Aurora**
오로라, 극광
We traveled to Norway to see the northern lights, the aurora.

**Backpacking**
배낭여행
He spent two months backpacking across Europe.

**Mesmerizing**
최면에 걸린 듯한, 매혹적인
The sunset over the ocean was absolutely mesmerizing.

**Nomad**
유목민; 떠돌이 생활을 하는 사람
Digital nomads can work from anywhere with a laptop and Wi-Fi.

**Culinary**
요리의, 음식과 관련된
She took a culinary class to improve her cooking skills.

**Companionship**
동반, 친구나 동료와의 교감
Traveling alone can be lonely, so I enjoy the companionship of friends.

**Get away from**
~에서 벗어나다, 떠나다
I need a vacation to get away from the busy city life.

**Skyscraper**
초고층 빌딩
The city skyline is full of impressive skyscrapers.
