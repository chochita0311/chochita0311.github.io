**1) Personal Goals**
- What personal goals are you working on right now?
- Have you ever set a goal and achieved it? What was it?
- Do you write down your personal goals? Why or why not?
- How do you stay motivated to reach your personal goals?
- What personal goal has changed your life the most?

**2) Career & Academic Goals**
- What career goals do you have for the next five years?
- How do you plan to reach your dream job?
- Have you ever changed your career goals? Why?
- What kind of academic goals did you have as a student?

**3) Lifestyle & Health Goals**
- How do you plan to maintain a healthy lifestyle?
- What’s one fitness goal you’ve achieved?
- Have you ever set a goal to break a bad habit?
- Have you ever reached a goal and then lost motivation?

▪️hard-earned
: 어렵게 얻은
"It was a hard-earned promotion after years of dedication."

▪️set a high standard
: 높은 기준을 세우다
"She set a high standard for future employees."

▪️career breakthrough
: 직업상의 돌파구
"That award was his career breakthrough."

▪️long-term achievement
: 장기적인 성취
"Writing a novel is a long-term achievement that takes patience."

▪️sense of fulfillment
: 충만감, 만족감
"Volunteering gives me a sense of fulfillment."

▪️push one’s limits
: 자신의 한계를 넘어서다
"He pushed his limits to climb the mountain."

▪️dream come true
: 꿈이 현실이 되다
"Studying abroad was a dream come true."

▪️earn a title
: 타이틀을 따다
"She earned the title of national champion."

▪️win an award
: 상을 받다
"The film won several international awards."

▪️be recognized for
: ~로 인정받다
"She was recognized for her leadership and hard work."
