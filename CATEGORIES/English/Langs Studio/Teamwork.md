**1) Teamwork & Collaboration**

**- What makes a team successful?**
work together → 함께 일하다
pull through → 어려움을 극복하다
cohesion → 결속
synergy → 시너지
on the same wavelength → 마음이 잘 맞는

_“A team becomes truly successful when its members are on the same wavelength and can pull through challenges with strong cohesion.”_

**- How do you usually contribute to a group project?**
step up → 앞장서다
pitch in → 돕다
initiative → 주도성
accountability → 책임감
pull your weight → 몫을 다하다

_“In group projects, I try to step up and pitch in wherever I can, because pulling my weight shows accountability.”_

**- What’s the hardest part of working in a team?**
deal with → 다루다
put up with → 참다
ego → 자존심
compromise → 타협
not on the same page → 생각이 안 맞는

_“The hardest part is dealing with big egos when people are not on the same page.”_

**- Do you prefer working alone or in a group?**
lean on → 의지하다
depend on → 의존하다
autonomy → 자율성
reliance → 의존
two heads are better than one (백지장도 맞들면 낫다)

_“Even though I enjoy autonomy, two heads are often better than one when solving tough problems.”_


**2) Motivation & Goals in Teams**

**- What motivates you the most in a team or group?**
look forward to → 기대하다
push through → 극복하다
recognition → 인정
collaboration → 협력
pulling in the same direction → 한 방향으로 힘을 합치는 것

_“What motivates me most is looking forward to collaboration where everyone pushes through obstacles, and recognition ensures we are all pulling in the same direction.”_

**- Do you think clear goals are always necessary for teams?**
lay out → 설명하다
stick to → 고수하다
direction → 방향
clarity → 명확성
crystal clear → 매우 명확한

_“Without crystal clear goals, teams can easily lose direction.”_

**- What keeps long-term projects moving forward?**
carry on → 계속하다
follow through → 끝까지 하다
commitment → 헌신
persistence → 끈기
keep the momentum → 추진력을 유지하다

_“Persistence and commitment help keep the momentum alive.”_

**- Have you ever had a personal breakthrough in a team?**
open up → 마음을 열다
level up → 향상되다
confidence → 자신감
discovery → 발견
lightbulb moment → 번뜩임의 순간

_“Working in a team gave me a lightbulb moment about my strengths.”_


**3) Team Experiences & Culture**

**- What’s the most memorable team you’ve ever been part of?**
look back on → 회상하다
stand out → 두드러지다
memories → 추억
bond → 유대
once-in-a-lifetime → 일생에 한 번뿐인

_“When I look back on my university debate team, it still stands out as a once-in-a-lifetime experience that shaped not only my confidence but also my lifelong friendships.”_

**- Have you ever been in a sports team?**
train for → 훈련하다
cheer on → 응원하다
discipline → 훈련
harmony → 조화
play your part → 역할을 다하다

_“Being in a soccer team taught me that no matter how skilled you are individually, the moment you fail to play your part, the entire strategy collapses.”_

**- How do you usually feel in large groups?**
blend in → 섞이다
stand out → 두드러지다
crowd → 군중
anxiety → 불안
lost in the crowd → 군중 속에서 사라지다

_“In large groups, I sometimes blend in too much and feel lost in the crowd, which makes it harder for my opinions to stand out.”_

**- Have you ever joined a volunteer team?**
give back → 돌려주다
sign up for → 참여하다
bond → 유대
impact → 영향
lend a helping hand → 도움을 주다

_“Joining a volunteer team gave me the chance to give back to society and lend a helping hand to people in real need.”_

▪️ collaboration
: 협력, 공동 작업
“Good collaboration makes the project successful.”
“좋은 협력이 프로젝트를 성공하게 만든다.”

▪️ division of roles
: 역할 분담
“The team had a clear division of roles.”
“팀은 명확한 역할 분담을 했다.”

▪️ team spirit
: 팀 정신
“Her team spirit encouraged everyone.”
“그녀의 팀 정신이 모두를 고무시켰다.”

▪️ shared responsibility
: 공동 책임
“Shared responsibility makes members more careful.”
“공동 책임은 팀원들을 더 신중하게 만든다.”

▪️ conflict resolution
: 갈등 해결
“Conflict resolution skills are essential in teamwork.”
“팀워크에서 갈등 해결 능력은 필수적이다.”

▪️ peer support
: 동료 지원
“Peer support helped him finish the tough task.”
“동료들의 지원 덕분에 그는 힘든 일을 끝낼 수 있었다.”

▪️ decision-making process
: 의사 결정 과정
“The decision-making process included every member.”
“의사 결정 과정에는 모든 팀원이 참여했다.”

▪️ consensus
: 합의, 의견 일치
“The team reached a consensus after discussion.”
“팀은 논의 끝에 합의에 도달했다.”

▪️ mutual respect
: 상호 존중
“Mutual respect makes teamwork smoother.”
“상호 존중은 팀워크를 원활하게 한다.”

▪️ accountability
: 책임 의식
“Each member has accountability for their task.”
“각 팀원은 자신의 일에 책임 의식을 가진다.”
