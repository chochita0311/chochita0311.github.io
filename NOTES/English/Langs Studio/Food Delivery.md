**1) Personal Experience & Habits**

**- How often do you order food delivery?**
order delivery → 배달을 시키다
frequency → 빈도
regularly → 정기적으로
once in a while → 가끔
almost every day → 거의 매일

**- What types of food do you usually order?**
fast food → 패스트푸드
Korean dishes → 한식
fusion → 퓨전 음식
healthy food → 건강식
comfort food → 위로가 되는 음식

**- Do you stick with one app or try different delivery services?**
stick with → ~을 고수하다
try out → 시험 삼아 사용하다
delivery app → 배달 앱
service fee → 서비스 수수료
user interface → 사용자 인터페이스

**- Do you ever have problems with food delivery?**
late delivery → 배달 지연
wrong order → 잘못된 주문
cold food → 식은 음식
missing item → 빠진 음식
customer service → 고객 서비스

**2) Convenience & Cost**

**- How do you decide between delivery, takeout, and eating out?**
takeout → 포장
dine out → 외식하다
mood → 기분
weather → 날씨
convenience → 편리함

**- Do you think food delivery is worth the extra cost?**
worth it → 그만한 가치가 있는
delivery charge → 배달 요금
tip → 팁
time-saving → 시간 절약
expensive → 비싼

_"Even though it's more expensive, I think food delivery is worth it when I'm too tired to cook."_

**- Do you think people rely too much on food delivery these days?**
rely on → ~에 의존하다
too convenient → 너무 편리한
daily habit → 일상 습관
health impact → 건강에 미치는 영향
addictive → 중독성 있는

**- Have you noticed any price differences between apps or restaurants?**
price difference → 가격 차이
markup → 가격 인상
discount → 할인
in-app promotion → 앱 내 프로모션
hidden fee → 숨겨진 요금

**3) Culture & Social Impact**

**- Is food delivery changing the way people cook and eat together?**
home-cooked meal → 집밥
family time → 가족 시간
eating habits → 식습관
solo dining → 혼밥
cook less → 덜 요리하다

_"Because of food delivery, some families may spend less time cooking and eating together."_

**- How do food delivery habits differ across countries or cultures?**
eating culture → 식문화
tipping → 팁 문화
local food → 지역 음식
delivery speed → 배달 속도
popular items → 인기 메뉴

**- Do you think delivery workers should be paid more or offered better protections?**
delivery worker → 배달 노동자
fair pay → 공정한 임금
working conditions → 근무 환경
safety gear → 안전 장비
employment rights → 고용 권리

_"Yes, delivery workers face many risks and should receive better treatment and protections."_

**- How has the food delivery industry changed since the pandemic?**
pandemic → 팬데믹
increase in demand → 수요 증가
contactless delivery → 비대면 배달
health concerns → 건강 우려
growth in platform → 플랫폼 성장

▪️order in
: 배달시켜 먹다
"We were too tired to cook, so we decided to order in."

▪️go-to delivery place
: 단골 배달집
"What's your go-to delivery place when you're craving something greasy?"

▪️late-night delivery
: 야식 배달
"Late-night delivery is dangerous—I end up eating at 2 a.m. way too often."

▪️delivery fee
: 배달비
"Some places charge a crazy delivery fee even if it's close by."

▪️minimum order amount
: 최소 주문 금액
"I had to add a drink to meet the minimum order amount."

▪️menu scroll
: 배달앱에서 뭘 먹을지 한참 고민하기
“I lost track of time during my late-night menu scroll — everything looked so good.”

▪️delivery splurge
: 배달 음식으로 돈 펑펑 쓰는 날
"I had a delivery splurge after my final exams."

▪️delivery guilt
: 배달음식 너무 자주 시켜서 생기는 죄책감
"This is my third delivery this week... I really need to cook."

▪️delivery fail
: 배달 대참사, 실패한 경험
"It was a total delivery fail — spilled soup and cold rice."

▪️leftovers tomorrow
: 배달음식 남아서 다음날 먹기
"The pizza was huge — I'm saving the rest for lunch tomorrow."
